Home Page Addendum

The tags that I used in home page are:

h1 - Used for Headings.
b - used for text to be in Bold.
i - used for text to be in Italic font.
u - used for text to be in Underline.
br - used to break the line in Paragragh.
p - used for the content to display in browser.
a href - used for link in Home Page to Article Page and Addendum Page.

Article Page Addendum

The tags that I used in Article Page are:

article - used for the page to be in format of article style.
footer - used for automatically update the last modified date of page.
